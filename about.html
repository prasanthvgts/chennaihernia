About The Team

Dr ( MAJOR ) R S Rengan MS, DNB, DLS ( FRANCE )

Dr (Major) R S Rengan is a National Board certified senior consultant Laparoscopic and General Surgeon in Chennai. He is considered one of the foremost hernia specialists in the country with a experience of more than 3 decades in both complex and simple hernia and abdominal wall surgery.

He has served on various committees that have aimed to standardise the teaching of hernia surgery for young surgeons. He has travelled all over the world including Europe and Taiwan to learn the specialised art of hernia surgery. He has served 15 years in the army and has worked in some of the finest institutions in the world.

He also specialises in Laparoscopic Gastrointestinal and Colo-rectal surgery. He has a special interest in Endocrine Surgery which includes Thyroid and Breast surgery. He has also performed a great number of laparoscopic gall bladder surgeries and often takes classes for budding surgeons on laparoscopic techniques and skills.

Dr Vinayak S Rengan MS, MRCS ( UK )

Dr Vinayak S Rengan is the junior consultant at Chennai Hernia Centre who specialises in laparoscopic management of hernias. He has trained in high volume centres of excellence in Chennai and Delhi. He works with his father Dr Rengan and is interested in laparoscopic oncology. He is a keen educationalist and academician who runs two popular online surgical education and training platforms surgtest.com and fellowshipcentral.org
