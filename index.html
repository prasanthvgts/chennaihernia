Chennai Hernia Centre

Chennai Hernia Centre (Best Hospital for Hernia Surgery Chennai) is an initiative by our Chief Surgeon Dr (Major) R S Rengan to create a hernia focussed clinic to ensure high standards of care without compromising on affordability in the field of hernia surgery. Dr (Major) R S Rengan is a renowned hernia surgeon who has performed thousands of ventral, inguinal, complex, and recurrent hernia surgeries on patients from all over the world.

His experience of more than 30 years in the field of open and laparoscopic surgery makes him one of the pioneers in the niche field of hernia surgery. Chennai hernia centre offers you options of open, laparoscopic and even mesh-free hernia surgery.

Dr Rengan has also performed thousands of hernia surgeries under local anaesthesia that allows patients to go home in a few hours after surgery. The focus of Chennai Hernia Centre is to offer you a solution that suits you. We realise that every patient is different and we tailor the solutions according to that
